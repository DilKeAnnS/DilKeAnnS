🌱 Stage 1: Foundation (0-1 Month)
▼
❓ WHAT
Identify your strength, interest, and market opportunity. Choose ONE business category - don't try multiple.
💰 Investment: ₹0-1,000 (only for research)
📋 CATEGORIES
Service Business (skill-based) | Product Business (physical goods) | Digital Business (online) | Franchise/Brick & Mortar
📚 LEARN BASICS
Marketing basics, sales skills, customer handling, basic finance, digital presence
🔗 FREE RESOURCES
YouTube (Business tutorials), Google Digital Garage, SWAYAM (NPTEL), Coursera (free audit), Udemy (discounted courses)
📋 Action Items for Stage 1
Week 1
Self-assessment - List your skills, interests, and resources
Week 2
Market research - Identify problem, target audience, competition
Week 3
Business model selection - Choose one category to focus on
Week 4
Basic learning - Complete 1-2 free courses on business basics
🧪 Stage 2: Skill Building (1-3 Months)
▼
🎯 HIGH-INCOME SKILLS
Graphic Design | Video Editing | Web Development | Digital Marketing | Content Creation | Copywriting | Social Media Management | App Development | Data Analytics | UI/UX Design
📚 FREE LEARNING
YouTube: FreeCodeCamp (Coding), HubSpot Academy (Marketing), Google Skillshop, Canva Design School, Coursera (free audit)
📁 PORTFOLIO
Create 5 sample works, 1 case study, basic website or Instagram page showcasing your skills
📋 Skill Building Roadmap
Month 1
Learn fundamentals - complete one comprehensive online course
Month 2
Practice - Create 5-10 sample projects/works for your portfolio
Month 3
Get feedback - Share your work online, join communities, improve based on feedback
End of Stage
Portfolio ready with 5+ projects, basic website/Instagram page created
🌿 Stage 3: Pick Your Business Model (1 Week)
▼
💼 Service Business (Fastest, Zero Investment)
Sell your skill - Graphic Design, Video Editing, Social Media Management, Freelance Writing, Virtual Assistant, Web Development, Digital Marketing, Teaching/Tutoring, Fitness Training, Makeup Artist
📦 Product Business (Low-Medium Investment)
Clothing/Accessories, Handmade Crafts, Food Products, Mobile Accessories, Home Decor, Custom Gifts, Stationery, Eco-friendly products, Ayurvedic products, Organic food
💻 Digital Business (Zero Cost)
YouTube Channel, Instagram Theme Pages, Affiliate Marketing, Dropshipping, Blogging, Digital Products (E-books, Templates, Courses), Print on Demand, NFTs
🏪 Franchise/Offline (₹30k-1L investment)
Tea Stall, Food Cart, Juice Centre, Cloud Kitchen, Printing & Stationery, Mobile Repair Shop, Beauty Salon, Tiffin Service, Grocery Store, Laundry Service
🏗 Stage 4: Build the Business (1-2 Months)
▼
🎨 CREATE IDENTITY
Business Name, Logo (Canva), Instagram Page, WhatsApp Business, Google Business Profile, Visiting Card (online printing), Basic Website (optional - free on Google Sites/Carrd)
🚀 START SMALL
Sell from home, provide services locally, freelance on Upwork/Fiverr, sell via WhatsApp/Instagram, list on Meesho/Amazon (for products)
🎯 FIRST 10 CUSTOMERS
Social media (Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn), School/College network, Local shops, Friends and relatives, Offer discounts, Provide value first (free samples/trials)
🌾 Stage 5: Grow (3-6 Months)
▼
📈 IMPROVE QUALITY
Faster delivery, better packaging, customer experience, professional communication, after-sales service
📢 MARKETING
Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, Google Ads (₹100-500/day), Facebook Ads, Local promotion, Posters in tuition/hostels/shops, Word of mouth referrals
🤝 BUILD TEAM
Part-timers, School/College interns, Freelancers from Fiverr/Upwork - delegate editing, social media, delivery, customer support
📈 Stage 6: Expand (6-12 Months)
▼
📝 REGISTER BUSINESS
Udyam Registration (Free, MSME), GST Registration (if turnover >₹40L), Trade License, FSSAI (for food business), Shop & Establishment Act
➕ ADD INCOME STREAMS
Clothing brand + accessories, Teaching + online courses, Photography + video shooting, Fitness training + online coaching
💰 APPLY FOR LOANS
Mudra Loan (₹50k-20L), Stand-Up India (₹10L-1Cr), PMEGP Subsidy, CGTMSE (collateral-free)
💪 Stage 7: Scale to Big Business (1-3 Years)
▼
⚙️ AUTOMATION
WhatsApp Business API, CRM (HubSpot free), Fb/Insta Ads automation, Email marketing automation, Inventory management software
🏆 BRAND TRUST
Consistent quality, Storytelling, Google Reviews collection, Partnerships with influencers, Sponsorships, Certifications
🚀 LONG-TERM VISION
Franchise model, Large online brand, Export business, High-end services, Agency model, Consultancy, IPO

🇮🇳 Central Government Schemes for Entrepreneurs
▼
🚀 Startup India Initiative
Benefits: 3-year tax exemption, self-certification compliance, IPR fast-track, access to Fund of Funds, easier procurement
Eligibility: DPIIT-registered startup (less than 10 years old)
🔗 startupindia.gov.in
💵 Startup India Seed Fund Scheme (SISFS)
Funding: ₹20-50 Lakhs for early-stage startups (prototype, trials, market entry)
Eligibility: DPIIT-registered, proof of concept
🔗 startupindia.gov.in
💰 Fund of Funds for Startups (FFS)
Corpus: ₹10,000 Crore | Government backs SEBI-registered AIFs/VCFs which invest in startups
Investment: Up to ₹25 Crore per startup
🔗 startupindia.gov.in
🛡️ Credit Guarantee Scheme for Startups (CGSS)
Collateral-free credit guarantee up to ₹10-20 Crore for startups sanctioned by banks/ NBFCs
🔗 nsdl.co.in
🏛️ Atal Innovation Mission (AIM)
By NITI Aayog - Grants and incubation support through Atal Incubation Centers (AICs), Atal Tinkering Labs (ATLs)
🔗 aim.gov.in
💻 TIDE 2.0 (MeitY)
For hardware, IoT, robotics, electronics startups - Grants up to ₹1 Cr + incubation support
🔗 tide.meity.gov.in
📊 Samridh Scheme (MeitY)
Funding up to ₹40 Lakhs + mentorship for software products and scalable solutions
🔗 meity.gov.in
🛡️ iDEX / Defence Innovation Scheme
Grants up to ₹1.5 Crore for defence and aerospace startups + incubation + industry linkage
🔗 idex.gov.in
🧬 BIRAC / BIO Grants
Non-dilutive funding up to ₹1 Crore for biotech & life sciences startups
🔗 birac.nic.in
🏭 MSME & Finance Support Schemes
▼
🏦 Pradhan Mantri Mudra Yojana (PMMY)
Collateral-free loans up to ₹20 Lakhs
Shishu (₹50k), Kishor (₹5 Lakhs), Tarun (₹10 Lakhs), Tarun Plus (₹20 Lakhs)
🔗 mudra.org.in
🇮🇳 PM Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP)
Subsidy 15-35% on bank loans for new manufacturing and service units (₹10L-25L)
🔗 pmegp.kviconline.gov.in
🎯 Stand-Up India Scheme
Loans ₹10 Lakhs-1 Crore for SC/ST and women entrepreneurs (greenfield enterprises)
🔗 standupmitra.in
🛡️ Credit Guarantee Fund Trust for Micro and Small Enterprises (CGTMSE)
Guarantees up to ₹5 Crore for MSME loans without collateral, reducing lending risk for banks
🔗 cgtmse.in
🌾 Agriculture Infrastructure Fund (AIF)
Loans up to ₹7.5 Crore with 3% interest subvention for agriculture infrastructure projects
🔗 agriinfra.dac.gov.in
⚡ 59-Minute Loan for MSME
Quick loan approval in principle from banks up to ₹5 Crore via simplified digital processing on PSB Loans portal
🔗 psbloansin59minutes.com
🏙️ State Government Schemes
▼
🏔️ Uttarakhand - Mukhyamantri Swarozgar Yojna
Loans up to ₹25 Lakhs (manufacturing), ₹10 Lakhs (services) + subsidy for entrepreneurs
🛠️ PM Vishwakarma / Vishwakarma Yuva Udyami Protsahan Yojana
Subsidised loans and support for artisans, craftspeople and youth entrepreneurs

💰 Complete Funding Guide for Startups
▼
🏦 Bootstrapping (Self-Funding)
Investment: ₹0-5L | Best for: Service businesses, freelancing, small ventures | Use savings, personal income, credit cards to fund initial operations.
👨‍👩‍👧 Friends & Family
Investment: ₹50k-10L | Best for: Early-stage startups | Borrow from friends/family. Keep clear terms, documentation, repayment schedule.
🏦 Mudra Loan (PMMY)
Loan Amount: ₹50k-20L | Interest: 8-12% | Tenure: 1-5 years | Collateral: Not required | Best for: Micro enterprises, small businesses
🏦 Stand-Up India Loan
Loan Amount: ₹10L-1Cr | Interest: 8-12% | Eligibility: SC/ST/Women entrepreneurs | Best for: Greenfield enterprises (new business)
🏦 PMEGP Subsidy Loan
Loan Amount: ₹10L-25L | Subsidy: 15-35% | Best for: Manufacturing and service units | Government contributes subsidy portion directly.
💳 Business Credit Card
Credit Limit: ₹50k-5L | Best for: Small expenses, building credit history | Useful for managing cash flow, tracking business expenses.
👼 Angel Investors
Investment: ₹20L-2Cr | Equity: 10-30% | Best for: Scalable startups with MVP/traction | Examples: Indian Angel Network, Mumbai Angels, Chennai Angels
🚀 Venture Capital (VC)
Investment: ₹2Cr-100Cr | Equity: 20-40% | Best for: High-growth startups | Stage: Series A, B, C | Examples: Sequoia, Accel, Lightspeed, Blume, Nexus
🎯 Crowdfunding
Platforms: Ketto, Milaap, Wishberry | Best for: Creative projects, social causes, product launches | Reward-based or donation-based (not equity)
📊 Bank Loans (MSME)
Loan Amount: ₹5L-5Cr | Interest: 9-14% | Tenure: 1-10 years | Collateral: May be required for larger amounts | SBI, HDFC, ICICI, PNB, BOB

▼

Cost: FREE | Time: 5-10 minutes | Required For: Govt schemes, bank loans, subsidies
🔗 udyamregistration.gov.in
🔢 GST Registration
Cost: FREE | Time: 5-7 days | Required For: Businesses with turnover >₹40L (goods) or >₹20L (services)
🔗 gst.gov.in
🏪 Shop & Establishment Act
Cost: ₹500-2,000 | Time: 7-15 days | Required For: All local shops, offices, commercial establishments (state-specific)

Cost: ₹1,000-7,500 | Time: 7-15 days | Required For: Food businesses (restaurants, tiffin, bakery, packaged food)
🔗 foscos.fssai.gov.in

Cost: ₹5,000-15,000 | Time: 15-30 days | Best For: Scalable startups, raising funding, partnership

🔗 dgft.gov.in

⚠️ 20 Common Mistakes New Entrepreneurs Make
▼
1
Starting without market research - Validate demand before investing money
2
No business plan - Failing to plan is planning to fail. Write it down.
3
Underestimating costs - Always add 30% buffer to initial budget
4
Mixing personal and business finances - Get separate bank account immediately
5
Trying to do everything yourself - Delegate, outsource, hire
6
Ignoring competition - Study competitors, learn from their mistakes
7
No marketing plan - Build audience before launch, not after
8
Focusing only on profit initially - Build value first, profit follows
9
Not understanding customers - Talk to customers regularly
10
Giving up too early - Most successful businesses took 3-5 years
11
No online presence - In 2025, digital presence is mandatory
12
Neglecting legal compliance - Get GST, MSME, tax filings done
13
Bad hiring decisions - Hire slow, fire fast
14
No customer feedback system - Listen to complaints, improve
15
Expanding too fast - Scale only when systems are ready
16
Not tracking metrics - What gets measured, gets improved
17
Fear of selling - Sales is serving, not manipulating
18
No emergency fund - Always keep 3-6 months of expenses
19
Not learning new skills - Business evolves, you must too
20
Surrounding with negative people - Join entrepreneur communities, get mentors

🌍 Global Business & Export Guide
▼
🌏 Why Export from India?
India has competitive advantage in: IT services, textiles, gems & jewellery, pharmaceuticals, auto components, spices, tea, coffee, organic products, handicrafts.
📋 Getting Started with Export
Step 1: Obtain IEC (Import Export Code) - ₹500, online
Step 2: Choose product and target market
Step 3: Find buyers via trade fairs, B2B platforms (Alibaba, IndiaMART), government trade missions
Step 4: Understand documentation (invoice, packing list, Bill of Lading, shipping documents)
🎯 Top Products India Exports
#1 Petroleum products | #2 Gems & Jewellery | #3 Pharma & Drugs | #4 Iron & Steel | #5 Textiles & Garments | #6 Auto Components | #7 Electronics | #8 Spices | #9 Organic Chemicals | #10 Marine Products
🏛️ Government Support for Exporters
FIEO (Federation of Indian Export Organisations) | DGFT (Directorate General of Foreign Trade) | Export Promotion Councils | MEIS/SEIS (Merchandise/Service Exports Incentive Schemes) | RoDTEP (Remission of Duties and Taxes on Exported Products)
