🌏 Study Abroad – International Careers
🌏 Abroad Studies Stream
🇺🇸 Study in the USA
• Popular courses: Business, Computer Science, Nursing, Engineering, Arts, Design, Psychology
• Pathway: 12th → SAT/IELTS/TOEFL → University → OPT Work Visa → H-1B
• Official: EducationUSA (state.gov)
🇨🇦 Study in Canada
• Top courses: Diploma in Business, Computer Science, Hospitality, Agriculture, Healthcare, Engineering, Trades
• Pathway: 12th → IELTS → Diploma/Bachelor → PGWP → PR
• Official: Canada.ca/study
🇬🇧 Study in the United Kingdom (UK)
• Courses: Business, Computer Science, Medicine (foundation), Law, Arts, Fashion, Design
• Pathway: 12th → IELTS → Foundation/Bachelor → Graduate Route → Work Visa
• Official: UK Government Student Visa
🇦🇺 Study in Australia
• Popular: Nursing, IT, Engineering, Agriculture, Hospitality, Automotive, Trades
• Pathway: 12th → IELTS/PTE → TAFE Diploma/Bachelor → PSW → PR options
• Official: StudyAustralia.gov.au
🇳🇿 Study in New Zealand
• Top: Agriculture, IT, Engineering Technicians, Hospitality, Film & Animation
• Pathway: 12th → IELTS → Diploma/Bachelor → Post-study work visa → PR
• Official: StudyWithNewZealand.govt.nz
🇩🇪 Study in Germany (Free/Low Cost)
• Engineering, Computer Science, Business, Design, Medicine (long duration)
• Requirements: German A1-B1 or English-taught programs with IELTS
• Official: DAAD Germany
🇫🇷 Study in France
• Fashion & Luxury, Art & Design, Business, Hospitality, Engineering, Culinary Arts
• Official: Campus France
🇮🇪 Study in Ireland
• Computer Science (high demand), Business, Nursing, Engineering
• Official: Education in Ireland
🇯🇵 Study in Japan
• Engineering, Robotics, Japanese Language Program, Animation, Gaming, Manga
• Official: StudyInJapan.go.jp
🇰🇷 Study in South Korea
• Korean Language Program, Engineering, Fashion, Film & Media, K-Arts
• Official: StudyInKorea.go.kr
🇦🇪 Study in UAE / Dubai
• Business, IT, Hospitality, Aviation Management
• Official: UAE Ministry of Education
🧭 Types of Courses After 12th
• Bachelor Degrees (3-4 years): BBA, BSc, Engineering, Nursing, IT, Arts, Design
• Diplomas (1-2 years): Business, IT, Hospitality, Agriculture, Automotive, Healthcare (popular for PR in Canada/Australia/NZ)
• Foundation Programs (1 year): to improve English, Math, academic readiness
💰 Cheapest Options for Indian Students
• Germany (public universities free), France (low tuition), Italy (scholarships), Poland, Hungary, Japan/Korea (language scholarships)
🎓 Full Scholarships After 12th
• Fulbright (USA), DAAD (Germany), MEXT (Japan), KGSP (South Korea), Campus France Scholarships
🌟 Which Country is Best For You?
• PR + Job opportunities → Canada, Australia, New Zealand
• Low budget education → Germany, France, Italy
• High-tech careers → USA, Japan, Korea
• Fashion / Design → France, Italy, UK
📋 General Admission Requirements
• 12th pass with minimum 60-70% (depends on university)
• English proficiency: IELTS (6.0-7.0), TOEFL (80-100), PTE
• Standardized tests: SAT (USA), GRE/GMAT (postgraduate)
• Statement of Purpose, Letters of Recommendation, Financial proof, Passport & Visa
