🗺️ Canadian PR Pathway (Express Entry)
Step 1
Complete your study program (minimum 8 months, preferably 2 years for longer PGWP)
Step 2
Apply for PGWP (Post-Graduation Work Permit) - up to 3 years duration
Step 3
Gain Canadian work experience (minimum 1 year in NOC TEER 0,1,2,3)
Step 4
Create Express Entry profile under Canadian Experience Class (CEC)
Step 5
Receive ITA (Invitation to Apply) based on CRS score
Step 6
Submit PR application → Receive Confirmation of Permanent Residence (COPR) → PR Card

📋 Entrance Exams for Abroad Studies - Complete Guide
▼
🇬🇧 IELTS (International English Language Testing System)
Accepted by: UK, Canada, Australia, NZ, USA, Ireland | Format: Listening, Reading, Writing, Speaking (3 hours) | Score: 6.0-7.5 | Fee: ₹16,250 | ielts.org
🇺🇸 TOEFL (Test of English as a Foreign Language)
Accepted by: USA, Canada, UK, Australia | Format: Reading, Listening, Speaking, Writing (3.5 hours) | Score: 80-110 | Fee: $190 | ets.org/toefl
🇦🇺 PTE (Pearson Test of English)
Accepted by: Australia, NZ, UK, Canada, Ireland | Format: Computer-based (3 hours) | Score: 50-79 | Fee: ₹14,700 | pearsonpte.com
🇺🇸 SAT (Scholastic Assessment Test)
For: Undergraduate admissions in USA and Canada | Format: Reading, Writing & Language, Math (3 hours) | Score: 400-1600 | Fee: $55 | collegeboard.org/sat
🇺🇸 GRE (Graduate Record Examination)
For: Graduate admissions in USA, Canada, UK, Australia | Format: Verbal, Quantitative, Analytical Writing (3.5 hours) | Score: 260-340 | Fee: $205 | ets.org/gre
🇺🇸 GMAT (Graduate Management Admission Test)
For: MBA programs worldwide | Format: Analytical Writing, Integrated Reasoning, Quantitative, Verbal (3.5 hours) | Score: 200-800 | Fee: $275 | gmac.com/gmat
🇩🇪 TestDaF (Test Deutsch als Fremdsprache)
For: German universities (German proficiency) | Format: Reading, Listening, Writing, Speaking | Score: TDN 3-5 | Fee: €195 | testdaf.de
🇫🇷 TEF/TCF (French proficiency)
For: French universities | Format: Reading, Listening, Speaking, Writing | Required level: B2/C1 | Fee: €200-300
