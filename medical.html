💡 Career Tips for Medical Aspirants

✅ Start NEET preparation from Class 11
✅ Focus on NCERT Biology (80% questions come from NCERT)
✅ Practice daily with mock tests
✅ Join coaching or online platforms like Unacademy, Byju's
✅ After MBBS, consider specialization (MD/MS) for higher salary
✅ Govt medical colleges have lower fees (₹50K/year vs ₹15L+ in private)
