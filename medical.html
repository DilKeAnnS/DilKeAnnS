🩺 MBBS - Bachelor of Medicine & Surgery (5.5 years)
▼
❓ WHAT
Professional medical degree to become a licensed doctor.
🌟 WHY
Highest respect, job security, ability to save lives, ₹8 LPA - ₹2 Cr+
⏰ WHEN
After 12th PCB (Age 17+). Start NEET prep from Class 11.
📍 WHERE
AIIMS Delhi, CMC Vellore, JIPMER, AFMC, Maulana Azad, Grant Medical
📋 WHICH EXAM
NEET (National Eligibility cum Entrance Test)
👤 WHOM
Hardworking, empathetic, good memory, stress management
🛠️ HOW
12th PCB → NEET → MBBS (5.5 years) → Internship → PG
📜 PAST
Limited seats (20,000), separate college exams
📊 PRESENT
NEET common gateway, 1 lakh+ seats, 18 lakh aspirants
🚀 FUTURE
AI diagnostics, robotic surgery, telemedicine, global opportunities
