🩺 MBBS - Bachelor of Medicine & Surgery
▼
❓ WHAT
MBBS is a professional undergraduate medical degree that trains students to become licensed medical doctors capable of diagnosing, treating, and preventing diseases.
🌟 WHY
Highest social respect, job security, ability to save lives, excellent income (₹8 LPA - ₹2 Cr+), endless learning opportunities
⏰ WHEN
After 12th PCB (Age 17+). Start NEET preparation from Class 11 itself.
📍 WHERE
AIIMS, CMC Vellore, JIPMER, AFMC, Maulana Azad, Grant Medical, KGMU, BHU
📋 WHICH EXAM
NEET (National Eligibility cum Entrance Test) - Only entrance for MBBS in India
👤 WHOM
Hardworking, empathetic, good memory, stress management, strong Biology & Chemistry interest
🛠️ HOW
12th PCB (50%+ General, 40% Reserved) → Clear NEET → MBBS (5.5 years) → Internship → PG → Super-specialisation
📜 PAST
Limited seats (20,000), separate college exams, very expensive private colleges
📊 PRESENT
NEET common gateway, 1 lakh+ seats, high competition (18 lakh students), telemedicine growth
🚀 FUTURE
AI-assisted diagnostics, robotic surgery, personalized medicine, geriatric care boom, international opportunities

🗺️ Complete Career Roadmap - MBBS to Super Specialist
Class
10-12
PCB Stream - Focus on Biology, Chemistry, Physics. Start NEET preparation from Class 11. Minimum 50% marks required for General category.
After
12th
NEET Exam - Clear NEET with good rank. General category needs 50th percentile (720-137 marks). SC/ST/OBC needs 40th percentile.
5.5 Years
MBBS Degree - 4.5 years academic + 1 year compulsory rotating internship. Learn medicine, surgery, pediatrics, gynecology, etc.
3 Years
PG (MD/MS) - Specialize in fields like Medicine (MD), Surgery (MS), Pediatrics, Radiology, etc. via NEET-PG. Salary after PG: ₹15-30 LPA.
3 Years
Super Specialization (DM/MCh) - For fields like Cardiology, Neurology, Gastroenterology, Urology. Top salary: ₹50 LPA - ₹2 Cr+.
Final
Consultant / Professor / HOD - Lead a department, teach future doctors, or open private practice. Highest achievement: Head of Department (HOD) at top institute.
💰 Scholarships for MBBS Students
PM Scholarship Scheme (PMSS) - For wards of Ex-Servicemen | ₹2,500/month
National Scholarship Portal (NSP) - Central/State scholarships | ₹5,000-50,000/year
INSPIRE Scholarship - For Science students (Top 1% in 12th) | ₹80,000/year
State Govt Scholarships - Check your state's scholarship portal

🦷 BDS - Bachelor of Dental Surgery
▼
❓ WHAT
BDS is the undergraduate degree for dental surgery, training students to become dentists.
🌟 WHY
High demand for dentists, flexible work hours, own clinic possible, good salary (₹6-30 LPA)
⏰ WHEN
After 12th PCB (Age 17+), same as MBBS
📍 WHERE
Maulana Azad Dental College, Government Dental College Mumbai, NIMS Dental College
📋 WHICH EXAM
NEET (same as MBBS)
👤 WHOM
Students interested in dental health, good hand-eye coordination, patient communication skills
🛠️ HOW
12th PCB → NEET → BDS (5 years) → Internship → MDS or Practice
🚀 FUTURE
Cosmetic dentistry, implantology, orthodontics, dental tourism in India
📋 NEET Same as MBBS - Cutoff same category-wise
