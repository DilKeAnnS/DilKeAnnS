🩺 MBBS - Bachelor of Medicine & Surgery (5.5 years)
▼
❓ WHAT
Complete medical degree covering anatomy, physiology, pharmacology, pathology, clinical practice, surgery.
💰 Fresher (JR): ₹6-12 LPA | Senior Resident: ₹12-20 LPA | Consultant: ₹20-60 LPA
🌟 WHY
Most respected profession, save lives, high earning potential, global demand, super-specialization options.
💰 Surgeon: ₹30-80 LPA | Cardiologist: ₹50L-2Cr
⏰ WHEN
After 12th PCB (Physics, Chemistry, Biology) with 50%+ (40% for SC/ST). Age 17+.
📍 WHERE
AIIMS Delhi | CMC Vellore | MAMC Delhi | JIPMER | KEM Mumbai
📋 EXAMS
NEET-UG (National Eligibility cum Entrance Test) | AIIMS (now through NEET)
👤 WHOM
Empathy, patience, strong memory, problem-solving, ability to work under pressure, passion for healing
🛠️ HOW
12th PCB → NEET → MBBS (5.5 yrs incl. 1 yr internship) → NEET-PG → MD/MS → Super-specialty (DM/MCh)

🗺️ Complete Career Roadmap (10th → Chief of Medicine)
Class 10-12
PCB focus, NEET preparation (11th & 12th), score 650+ for top colleges.
💰 After NEET: MBBS seat
5.5 Years
MBBS (4.5 years academics + 1 year internship)
💰 Internship stipend: ₹15-25k/month
3 Years

💰 PG Resident: ₹12-18 LPA

Consultant → Head of Department → Professor

Final
Chief of Medical Services / Director of Hospital / Private Super-specialty Practice

💊 Pharmacy - B.Pharm & D.Pharm (4 years / 2 years)
▼
❓ WHAT
Drug formulation, pharmacology, pharmaceutical chemistry, clinical pharmacy, pharmacovigilance.
💰 Fresher: ₹3-6 LPA | Pharmacist: ₹4-8 LPA | Drug Inspector: ₹12-20 LPA | M.Pharm: ₹7-15 LPA
🌟 WHY
Indian pharma industry is world's 3rd largest, high demand in R&D, quality control, regulatory affairs, clinical research.
📋 EXAMS
GPAT for M.Pharm, state pharmacy entrance exams. Top colleges: NIPER, Jamia Hamdard, BITS Pilani, Manipal, Poona College of Pharmacy.
🗺️ Pharmacy Career Roadmap
2-4 Years
D.Pharm (2 yrs) / B.Pharm (4 yrs)
💰 Pharmacist: ₹3-6 LPA
2 Years
M.Pharm (Pharmaceutics, Pharmacology, Pharmaceutical Chemistry, Regulatory Affairs)
💰 M.Pharm: ₹7-15 LPA
Career Paths
Pharma Industry (R&D, QA/QC, Production), Clinical Research (CRO), Drug Inspector (Govt), Pharmacovigilance, Medical Writing, Teaching, PhD, Own Pharmacy Store

📚 NEET-UG Syllabus & Pattern
Physics
Class 11 & 12: Mechanics, Thermodynamics, Optics, Modern Physics (45 questions)
Chemistry
Physical, Organic, Inorganic: Periodic table, Chemical bonding, Biomolecules (45 questions)
Biology
Botany + Zoology: Genetics, Human Physiology, Ecology, Evolution (90 questions)
🎯 Total
180 questions | 720 marks | 3 hours 20 minutes | Negative marking (-1 for wrong)
💡 NEET Preparation Tips (Dil-Ke-AnnS Guide)
Class 11
Complete 50% syllabus, NCERT focus, basic problem-solving
Class 12
Complete remaining syllabus, revision, mock tests, previous year papers
Dropper
Intensive coaching, daily 8-10 hours study, 50+ mock tests, analyze mistakes
