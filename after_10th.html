📖 Complete Guide After 10th Class
🎓 After 10th Career Roadmap
🔬 Science Stream (PCM / PCB)
• Best for: Engineering, Medical, Defence, IT, Research, Aviation
• PCM (Physics, Chemistry, Maths) → Engineering, Defence, BCA, B.Sc, Pilot
• PCB (Physics, Chemistry, Biology) → Medical, Nursing, Pharmacy, Veterinary
📊 Commerce Stream
• Best for: Business, Banking, Finance, CA, CS, Economics
• After 12th → B.Com, BBA, CA, CS, CMA, CFA, LLB, Hotel Management
🎨 Arts / Humanities Stream
• Best for: UPSC, Law, Journalism, Psychology, Social Work
• After 12th → BA, LLB, BJMC, BSW, BFA, Civil Services
🔧 Polytechnic Diploma (3 Years)
• Direct career path after 10th – skip 11th/12th
• Branches: Mechanical, Electrical, Civil, Computer, Automobile, Electronics
• After diploma: Direct job OR lateral entry to B.Tech 2nd year
🛠️ ITI – Skill Based Jobs (1-2 Years)
• Electrician, Fitter, Welder, Diesel Mechanic, AC Technician, Plumber
• Carpenter, Automobile Mechanic, CCTV Technician, Computer Operator
• Fast jobs in Private & Government sector
🩺 Paramedical & Healthcare
• ANM Nursing, GNM Nursing, Medical Lab Assistant, X-Ray Technician
• Home Health Aide, Emergency Medical Technician (EMT)
💻 Vocational Courses (Skill India)
• Graphic Design, Animation & VFX, Digital Marketing, Photography
• App Development, Beauty & Wellness, Retail Management
• Quick part-time or full-time jobs after 3-12 months
🏅 Sports Pathway
• Sports School / Academy, Fitness Trainer, Physiotherapy Assistant
• Sports Management, Government Sports Quota Jobs
• Choose 11th/12th with Physical Education
🏛️ Government Jobs After 10th
• SSC MTS, Railway Group D, Police Home Guard
• Army GD (17.5 years), Navy MR, Post Office Jobs, State Govt Jobs
🌍 Abroad Study Path
• Choose Science/Commerce/Arts → Study Abroad after 12th
• Fields: Nursing, IT, Engineering, Management, Hotel Management
• Countries: USA, Canada, UK, Australia, Germany, Japan, Korea
💡 Key Tips for After 10th
✅ Choose stream based on YOUR interest and strengths, not peer pressure
✅ Vocational/Diploma/ITI are excellent for early employment
✅ You can switch streams later – many options exist
✅ Keep a backup plan – skill courses alongside regular studies
✅ Start exploring career options from Class 9 itself
✅ Don't compare your journey with others – everyone has their own path
