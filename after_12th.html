🔬 Science Stream (PCM/PCB) - Complete Overview
▼
❓ WHAT
Study of Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Mathematics. Foundation for Engineering, Medical, Research, IT, Defence, Aviation, and Pure Sciences.
💰 Starting Salary: ₹4-8 LPA | After PG: ₹8-25 LPA | After PhD: ₹12-40 LPA
🌟 WHY
Most career options, highest earning potential, gateway to IITs/NITs/AIIMS, opportunities in PSUs (ISRO, DRDO, BHEL), global job market, research opportunities.
📋 SUBJECTS
Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics (PCM) OR Biology (PCB), English, optional subjects

💡 Important Tips for After 12th Students
▼
🎯 Tip 1
Choose your career path based on YOUR interest, aptitude, and market demand - not family or peer pressure.
🎯 Tip 2
Professional courses (CA, CS, CMA) can be done alongside graduation - plan your time wisely.
🎯 Tip 3
Skill courses (Digital Marketing, Web Development, Data Science) can provide side income even during studies.
🎯 Tip 4
Government exam preparation requires discipline - start early, follow a routine, take mock tests regularly.
🎯 Tip 5
Gap year is OK if used productively - internship, skill course, entrance exam preparation, or volunteering.
🎯 Tip 6
Build a professional network on LinkedIn - connect with alumni, professors, and industry professionals.
🎯 Tip 7
Learn English and computer skills - essential for almost every job and higher education.
🎯 Tip 8
Use free resources - YouTube, NPTEL, SWAYAM, Coursera (audit), edX (audit), Khan Academy.
