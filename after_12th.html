🎯 Complete Guide After 12th Class
🎓 After 12th Career Roadmap
🩺 Medical Stream (PCB)
• MBBS, BDS, BAMS, BHMS, BUMS, BNYS
• B.Sc Nursing, BPT, BOT, Radiology, MLT, OT Technology
• B.Pharm, Pharm.D, D.Pharm
• BVSc & AH – Veterinary Science
• Entrance: NEET – Mandatory for MBBS/BDS
⚙️ Non-Medical / Engineering (PCM)
• B.Tech/B.E in CSE, Mechanical, Civil, Electrical, AI/ML, Data Science, Robotics, Aerospace
• B.Arch, BCA, B.Sc Computer Science, Merchant Navy, NDA, Pilot Training
• Entrance: JEE Main, JEE Advanced, BITSAT, VITEEE, NATA
📊 Commerce Stream
• B.Com, BBA, BAF, BBI, BFM, Economics
• Professional: CA, CS, CMA, CFA
• Law: LLB (5 years) through CLAT
🎨 Arts / Humanities Stream
• BA in Political Science, History, Geography, Psychology, Sociology, English
• BA+LLB, BBA, BJMC, BFA, BSW, BHM, B.Ed
• Best for: UPSC, Law, Journalism, Psychology, Social Work
🌾 Agriculture & Life Sciences
• B.Sc Agriculture, Horticulture, Forestry, Fisheries, Food Technology
• B.Tech Agricultural Engineering, Biotechnology, Dairy Technology
• Entrance: ICAR AIEEA, State Agriculture Exams
📜 CA – Chartered Accountant
• Path: CA Foundation → Intermediate → Articleship (3 years) → Final
• Salary: ₹8-30 LPA after qualification
📜 CS – Company Secretary
• Path: CSEET → Executive → Professional
• Salary: ₹6-20 LPA
📜 CMA – Cost & Management Accountant
• Path: Foundation → Intermediate → Final
• Salary: ₹6-15 LPA
📜 CFA – Chartered Financial Analyst (Global)
• Path: Level 1 → Level 2 → Level 3
• Salary: ₹8-35 LPA (international)
🎨 Designing & Creative Fields
• B.Des (Fashion, Interior, Graphic, UI/UX, Animation, Product)
• Entrance: NIFT, NID DAT, UCEED
🏨 Hotel Management
• BHM – Bachelor of Hotel Management
• Careers: Hotel Manager, Chef, Event Planner, Airline Services
🏛️ Government Jobs After 12th
• SSC CHSL (10+2 level), SSC GD, Railway RRB (Group D, NTPC)
• Police Constable, Forest Guard, ESIC, FCI, LIC Assistant
• After graduation: UPSC, SSC CGL, Banking PO, State PSC
🛡️ Defence & Armed Forces
• NDA (Army/Navy/Air Force) – After 12th
• TES – Technical Entry Scheme, Indian Army
• Agniveer, Coast Guard, Paramilitary Forces (CRPF, BSF, ITBP)
• Sports quota and NCC special entry
🏦 Banking Jobs
• IBPS PO/Clerk, SBI PO, RBI Grade B
• Salary: ₹8-15 LPA for PO
🌍 Study Abroad After 12th
• Engineering (B.S), Computer Science, Business Management, Nursing, Hospitality
• Top countries: USA, Canada, UK, Australia, Germany, New Zealand, Ireland, Japan
• Requirements: IELTS/TOEFL, SAT/ACT, SOP, LORs
• Scholarships: Fulbright, DAAD, MEXT, KGSP
💼 Direct Jobs & Entrepreneurship
• BPO/KPO, Retail, Sales, Real Estate, Insurance, Banking (customer service)
• Freelancing: Content writing, Graphic design, Web development, Digital marketing
• Start your own business: E-commerce, Coaching centre, Food outlet, YouTube channel
💡 Key Tips for After 12th
✅ Choose based on YOUR interest and market demand – not family pressure
✅ Professional courses (CA, CS) can be done alongside graduation
✅ Skill courses (Digital Marketing, Web Dev) give side income during studies
✅ Government exam preparation requires discipline – start early, follow routine
✅ Gap year is OK if used productively (internship, skill course, entrance prep)
