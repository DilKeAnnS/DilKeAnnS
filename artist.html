• BA in Drama & Theatre Arts – Scriptwriting, Stagecraft, Direction
• Diploma/Certificate in Acting – 3 to 12 months, practical training
• Careers: Stage actor, Film/TV actor, Theatre director, Drama coach, Voice artist
💃 Dance & Performing Arts
• BPA / BA in Dance – Classical (Kathak, Bharatanatyam, Odissi), Contemporary, Bollywood, Hip-hop
• Diploma in Classical / Contemporary Dance
• Careers: Professional dancer, Choreographer, Dance teacher, Stage performer
🎵 Music & Singing
• BPA / BA in Music – Vocal, Instrumental (Guitar, Piano, Tabla, etc.)
• Diploma in Singing / Music Performance, Music Technology / Sound Engineering
• Certificates: Playback singing, Classical vocal, Instrument training
• Careers: Singer, Music producer, Playback artist, Sound engineer, Music teacher
🎬 Film & Media (Behind the Scenes)
• B.Sc / BA in Film & Television Production
• B.Des in Film & Video Communication, BFA in Cinema & Visual Arts
• Diploma: Film editing, Cinematography, Screenwriting, Video production
• Careers: Film director, Cinematographer, Editor, Screenwriter, Producer
🎭 Bachelor of Performing Arts (BPA) – All-in-One
