🎭 Acting & Theatre - Complete Career Guide
▼
❓ WHAT
Study of acting techniques, theatre production, voice modulation, body language, script analysis, character development, stagecraft, and film acting.
💰 Fresher Actor: ₹3-8 LPA | TV Actor: ₹10-30 LPA | Film Actor: ₹50 LPA - ₹100 Cr+
🌟 WHY
Creative expression, fame, recognition, financial success, opportunity to tell important stories, work with talented artists, global recognition.
⏰ WHEN
After 12th (any stream). Start early with theatre workshops, school plays, drama clubs. Age 17+ for formal courses.
📍 WHERE
NSD Delhi (National School of Drama) | FTII Pune | Whistling Woods Mumbai | Asian Academy of Film & TV
📋 COURSES
BA in Acting (3 years), Diploma in Acting (1-2 years), Certificate in Acting (3-12 months), BPA (Bachelor of Performing Arts), PG Diploma in Acting
👤 WHOM
Passion for performance, creativity, emotional range, confidence, good memory, physical fitness, ability to take direction
🛠️ HOW
12th → Acting Entrance Exam (NSD/FTII/Whistling Woods) → Acting Course (1-4 years) → Theatre/Film/TV/Web Series → Audition → Role
📋 Top Acting Entrance Exams
🎭 NSD Entrance
National School of Drama - 3-year Diploma | Audition based | nsd.gov.in
🎬 FTII Acting Course
Film and Television Institute of India - PG Diploma | ftii.ac.in

Mumbai - BA in Acting | whistlingwoods.net

Banaras Hindu University - BPA | bhu.ac.in

🎨 BFA - Bachelor of Fine Arts (4 years) - Complete Career Guide
▼
❓ WHAT
Study of painting, sculpture, printmaking, applied arts, digital art, ceramics, glass art, installation art, art history, and visual communication.
💰 BFA Fresher: ₹4-8 LPA | MFA: ₹8-15 LPA | Art Director: ₹15-30 LPA | Gallery Owner: ₹20-50 LPA
🌟 WHY
Creative expression, art market growth (India's art market is ₹2,000+ crore), gallery exhibitions, art auctions (Saffronart, Christie's), freelance opportunities, art teaching.
📍 WHERE
College of Art Delhi | Kala Bhavana Santiniketan | Jamia Millia Islamia - Faculty of Fine Arts | Sir JJ College of Arts Mumbai | MS University Baroda - Faculty of Fine Arts
📋 EXAMS
CUET, BFA Entrance Exam (College of Art Delhi), JMI Entrance, MSU Baroda Entrance
👤 WHOM
Creativity, imagination, visual thinking, patience, attention to detail, passion for art, ability to express through visual medium
🛠️ HOW
12th → BFA Entrance → BFA (4 years) → MFA (2 years) → PhD (3-5 years) → Artist/Professor
