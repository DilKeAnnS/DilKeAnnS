👗 Fashion Design - Complete Career Guide (4 years)
▼
❓ WHAT
Study of clothing design, textile science, fashion illustration, pattern making, garment construction, fashion marketing, merchandising, fashion forecasting.
💰 Fresher: ₹4-10 LPA | Senior Designer: ₹10-25 LPA | Fashion Director: ₹20-50 LPA | Celebrity Designer: ₹50 LPA - ₹5 Cr+
🌟 WHY
Indian fashion industry is ₹1,00,000+ crore. Global recognition, celebrity styling, export houses, luxury brands, own label opportunities.
⏰ WHEN
After 12th (any stream). Minimum 50% marks. Age 17+.
📍 WHERE
NIFT Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Chennai, Kolkata | Pearl Academy Delhi, Mumbai, Jaipur | NID Ahmedabad | Symbiosis Pune
📋 EXAMS
NIFT Entrance Exam | NID DAT | Pearl Academy Entrance | SEED (Symbiosis Entrance)
👤 WHOM
Creativity, fashion awareness, sketching ability, attention to detail, trend forecasting, sewing skills, color sense
🛠️ HOW
12th → NIFT/NID Entrance → B.Des/B.Sc Fashion Design (4 years) → Internship → Job/Own Label → Fashion Director/CEO

📱 UI/UX Design - Complete Career Guide (3-4 years)
▼
❓ WHAT
User Interface (UI) design focuses on visual appearance, buttons, icons, spacing. User Experience (UX) design focuses on user research, wireframing, prototyping, user testing, information architecture.
💰 Fresher: ₹5-10 LPA | UI/UX Designer: ₹8-18 LPA | Senior UX Designer: ₹15-30 LPA | Product Design Lead: ₹25-50 LPA | UX Director: ₹40-80 LPA
🌟 WHY
Highest paying design specialization. High demand in tech industry, startups, product-based companies, remote work opportunities, global market.
📋 SOFTWARE
Figma, Adobe XD, Sketch, InVision, Miro, Balsamiq, Axure, Zeplin
