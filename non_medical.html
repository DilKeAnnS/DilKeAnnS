💻 CSE - Computer Science Engineering (4 years)
▼
❓ WHAT
Study of programming, algorithms, software development, computer systems, databases, networking, cybersecurity.
💰 Fresher Salary: ₹8-40 LPA
🌟 WHY
Highest paying engineering branch, global opportunities, work from home options, AI/ML/Data Science boom, high demand in IT sector.
💰 After M.Tech: ₹15-60 LPA
⏰ WHEN
After 12th PCM (Age 17+). Minimum 50-75% marks depending on college. Start JEE preparation from Class 11.
📍 WHERE
IIT Bombay | IIT Delhi | IIT Madras | NIT Trichy | BITS Pilani | VIT Vellore | SRM Chennai
📋 EXAMS
JEE Main | JEE Advanced | BITSAT | VITEEE | SRMJEE
👤 WHOM
Logical thinking, problem-solving ability, interest in coding and mathematics, creativity, patience for debugging, team player
🛠️ HOW
12th PCM → JEE Main/Advanced → B.Tech CSE (4 years) → M.Tech/MS or Job → Higher Studies or Industry

🤖 AI & ML - Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning (4 years)
▼
❓ WHAT
Study of algorithms that enable computers to learn from data and make decisions without explicit programming. Includes Deep Learning, Neural Networks, NLP, Computer Vision.
💰 Fresher Salary: ₹10-50 LPA
🌟 WHY
Fastest growing field in tech, AI revolution across all industries, highest salaries, global demand, future-proof career.
💰 After M.Tech/AI: ₹20-70 LPA
⏰ WHEN
After 12th PCM (Age 17+). Strong mathematics background required.
📍 WHERE
IIT Bombay | IIIT Hyderabad | BITS Pilani | IIIT Delhi
📋 EXAMS
JEE Main, JEE Advanced, BITSAT, VITEEE (same as CSE)
👤 WHOM
Strong mathematical aptitude, interest in statistics and probability, analytical thinking, curiosity about how AI works
🛠️ HOW
12th PCM → JEE → B.Tech AI/ML (4 years) → M.Tech/MS in AI/ML (2 years) → AI Researcher/Engineer
