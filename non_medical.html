💻 CSE - Computer Science Engineering (4 years)
▼
❓ WHAT
Study of programming, algorithms, software development, computer systems.
💰 Fresher Salary: ₹8-40 LPA
🌟 WHY
Highest paying engineering branch, global opportunities, work from home options.
💰 After M.Tech: ₹15-60 LPA
⏰ WHEN
After 12th PCM (Age 17+). Start JEE preparation from Class 11.
📍 WHERE
IIT Bombay | IIT Delhi | IIT Madras | NIT Trichy | BITS Pilani
📋 EXAM
JEE Main | JEE Advanced | BITSAT | VITEEE
👤 WHOM
Logical thinking, problem-solving, interest in coding, mathematics
🛠️ HOW
12th PCM → JEE Main/Advanced → B.Tech CSE (4 years) → M.Tech/MS or Job
