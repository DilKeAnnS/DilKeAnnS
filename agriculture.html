🌾 Agriculture & Allied Sciences
🌾 Agriculture Stream
🎓 After 10th – Diploma & ITI Options
• Diploma in Agriculture, Horticulture, Organic Farming, Food Processing, Seed Technology, Animal Husbandry, Poultry, Farm Management
• ITI Courses: Crop Production, Fruit & Vegetable Processing, Dairying, Food Technology, Horticulture, Soil Testing, Agricultural Machinery
• Certificate Courses: Organic Farming, Nursery Management, Beekeeping, Vermicomposting, Mushroom Cultivation, Dairy Management
• Career after Diploma: Agriculture Supervisor, Farm Manager, Agri-Technician, Irrigation Technician, Fertilizer/Pesticide Company jobs
🎓 After 12th – Bachelor Degrees
• B.Sc Agriculture (4 years) – most preferred for govt jobs
• B.Sc Horticulture, Forestry, Food Technology, Animal Husbandry, Fisheries (B.FSc), Dairy Science, Sericulture, Seed Technology, Plant Pathology, Soil Science, Agricultural Economics
• B.Tech Agricultural Engineering, Food Technology, Dairy Technology, Biotechnology, Agricultural IT
🐄 Veterinary & Allied
• Bachelor of Veterinary Science (BVSc & AH)
• B.Sc Animal Husbandry, Poultry Science, Dairy Technology
📊 Agribusiness & Management
• BBA in Agribusiness Management, B.Com Agriculture Business, B.Sc Agricultural Business Management
🛠️ Skill-Based Short Courses
• Beekeeping, Mushroom Cultivation, Nursery Management, Vermicomposting, Organic Farming, Soil Testing, Fertilizer Technology, Dairy Farming
🏛️ Government Jobs (Best for B.Sc Agriculture)
• Agriculture Officer, Rural Development Officer, Food Safety Officer, Agriculture Field Officer (AFO – Banks), NABARD, IFFCO, FCI, ICAR, State Agriculture Dept, Gram Sevak, Horticulture Assistant
🏢 Private Sector & Entrepreneurship
• Seed, fertilizer, pesticide companies, Agri-tech startups, Dairy plants, Food processing units, Organic farming business, Mushroom farming, Dairy/Poultry/Fish farming, Plant nursery, Beekeeping, Food processing (pickles, chips, fruit pulp)
🌍 Abroad Options
• Diploma in Agriculture (Canada, Australia, New Zealand), Bachelors in Horticulture/Forestry/Agri-Business, Farm worker → PR pathways
📋 Entrance Exams & Scholarships
• ICAR AIEEA (for B.Sc Agriculture), State Agriculture Entrance Exams, ICAR Scholarships
