📜 CA - Chartered Accountant
▼
❓ WHAT
Professional in accounting, auditing, taxation, financial management.
💰 Starting Salary: ₹8-15 LPA
🌟 WHY
Most respected commerce profession, highest earning potential.
💰 Experienced CA: ₹20-50 LPA | Partner: ₹50 LPA - ₹2 Cr
📋 EXAM
ICAI Exams | CA Foundation | CA Intermediate | CA Final
👤 WHOM
Detail-oriented, analytical, strong mathematics, discipline

Final
CA Qualification → Audit/Accounting/Tax/Consulting
