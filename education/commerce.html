📜 CA - Chartered Accountant (Complete Career Guide)
▼
❓ WHAT
Professional in accounting, auditing, taxation, financial management, corporate law.
💰 Starting Salary: ₹8-15 LPA | Experienced: ₹20-50 LPA | Partner: ₹50L-2Cr
🌟 WHY
Most respected commerce profession, highest earning potential, global recognition, statutory authority, mandatory for company audits, Big 4 opportunities (Deloitte, PwC, EY, KPMG).
⏰ WHEN
After 12th (register for CA Foundation) or after graduation (direct entry route). Age 17+.
📋 EXAM
ICAI (Institute of Chartered Accountants of India) - Official Website
👤 WHOM
Detail-oriented, analytical, strong mathematics, discipline, integrity, hard work, perseverance
🗺️ CA Complete Roadmap (4-5 Years)
4 Months
CA Foundation (4 papers: Principles of Accounting, Business Laws, Quantitative Aptitude, Business Economics)
📅 Exams: May/Nov | Pass %: 15-20% | Registration: After 12th
8 Months
CA Intermediate (8 papers - 2 groups: Accounting, Corporate Laws, Cost Management, Taxation, Advanced Accounting, Auditing, Financial Management, Information Systems)
📅 Exams: May/Nov | Pass %: 10-15%
2.5 Years
Articleship (Practical Training) - Work under a practicing CA. Get hands-on experience in audit, taxation, accounting.
💰 Stipend: Year 1: ₹2-5k/month | Year 2: ₹5-10k/month | Year 3: ₹10-20k/month
6 Months
CA Final (8 papers - 2 groups: Financial Reporting, Strategic Financial Management, Advanced Auditing, Corporate Laws, Direct Tax, Indirect Tax, Management Accounting)
📅 Exams: May/Nov | Pass %: 5-10%
Qualified
CA Member of ICAI → Practice or Job
💰 CA Fresher: ₹8-15 LPA | Senior CA: ₹20-50 LPA | Partner at CA Firm: ₹50L-2Cr

📋 CS - Company Secretary (Complete Career Guide)
▼
❓ WHAT
Expert in corporate law, governance, compliance, board management, securities law, and company regulations.
💰 Starting Salary: ₹6-12 LPA | Experienced: ₹15-30 LPA | Company Secretary in Listed Company: ₹25-50 LPA
🌟 WHY
Mandatory for listed companies, high demand in corporate sector, exposure to board meetings, prestigious profession, global recognition through ICSI.
⏰ WHEN
After 12th (any stream) or after graduation. Age 17+.
📋 EXAM
ICSI (Institute of Company Secretaries of India) - Official Website
👤 WHOM
Interest in corporate law, governance, attention to detail, good communication skills, integrity
🗺️ CS Complete Roadmap (3-4 Years)
CSEET
Company Secretary Executive Entrance Test (online, open book) - 4 papers
📅 Exams: Every month | Pass %: 30-40%
9 Months
CS Executive (8 papers - 2 modules: Jurisprudence, Company Law, Securities Law, Tax, Cost Management, Accounting, Economic Laws)
📅 Exams: June & December | Pass %: 10-15%
15 Months
CS Professional (9 papers - 3 modules: Governance, Ethics, Drafting, Due Diligence, Insolvency)
📅 Exams: June & December | Pass %: 8-12%
Training
15 months practical training in a company/CS firm
💰 Stipend: ₹5-15k/month
Qualified
CS Member of ICSI → Company Secretary at Corporate
💰 CS Fresher: ₹6-12 LPA | Experienced CS: ₹15-30 LPA | VP/Legal Head: ₹30-60 LPA
