🇮🇳 PMKVY - Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (Skill India)
▼
❓ WHAT
Government of India's flagship skill development program for free vocational training to youth.
💰 Training: FREE | Certification: Government recognized | Stipend: ₹500-2,000/month (for some courses)
🌟 WHY
No fees, government certification, placement assistance, recognition across India, direct industry linkage.
⏰ WHEN
After 8th, 10th, or 12th. Age 14-35 years. Multiple batches throughout the year.
🔗 WEBSITE
pmkvyofficial.org | nsdcindia.org

🗺️ How to Apply for PMKVY
Step 1
Visit pmkvyofficial.org or nearest PMKVY Training Center
Step 2
Register with Aadhaar card, choose your trade
Step 3
Complete training (3-12 months depending on trade)
Step 4
Pass assessment and get NSDC certification
Step 5
Receive placement assistance through PMKVY

🛠️ ITI - Industrial Training Institute (1-2 years) - Complete Guide
▼
❓ WHAT
Post-secondary vocational training for technical and non-technical trades. Government and private ITIs across India.
💰 Fresher: ₹12k-25k/month | Experienced: ₹20k-50k/month | Govt ITI: ₹30k-60k/month
🌟 WHY
Quick job placement, government jobs (Railways, Defence, PSUs), high demand for skilled workers, opportunity to start own business, NCVT certification recognized nationwide.
⏰ WHEN
After 8th, 10th, or 12th. Age 14-40 years. Admission through state-level CET.
📋 EXAMS
State ITI Entrance Exam (CET), Direct admission in private ITIs
🔗 OFFICIAL
dgt.gov.in (Directorate General of Training)

📋 NAPS - National Apprenticeship Promotion Scheme
▼
❓ WHAT
Government scheme providing on-the-job training with stipend. 75% of stipend paid by government.
💰 Stipend: ₹6,000-10,000/month | Government covers 75%
🌟 WHY
Earn while learning, direct industry exposure, high chance of permanent absorption, government support, recognized certificate.
🔗 WEBSITE
apprenticeshipindia.gov.in
🏛️ Top Companies Offering Apprenticeship
Indian Railways BHEL NTPC ONGC Maruti Suzuki Tata Motors L&T Mahindra Airports Authority of India Metro Rail Corporations
⚙️ Polytechnic Diploma (3 years) - Complete Guide
▼
❓ WHAT
3-year technical diploma after 10th. Lateral entry to B.Tech 2nd year possible.
💰 Fresher: ₹15k-30k/month | Experienced: ₹25k-60k/month | PSU: ₹40k-80k/month
📋 BRANCHES
Mechanical, Civil, Electrical, Computer Science, Electronics, Automobile, Chemical, Marine, Mining, Metallurgy, Instrumentation, Textile, Production, Ceramic, Food Technology
📋 EXAMS
JEECUP (UP), DCET (Karnataka), TS POLYCET, AP POLYCET, Punjab PET, JEXPO (West Bengal)
🗺️ ROADMAP
10th → Polytechnic (3 years) → Job → Lateral Entry to B.Tech (2 years) → B.Tech Degree → PSU/Govt Jobs
