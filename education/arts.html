▼
❓ WHAT
Study of humanities and social sciences - Political Science, History, Geography, Psychology, Sociology, Economics, English, Hindi, Sanskrit, Philosophy, Anthropology, Public Administration.
💰 Fresher Salary: ₹3-7 LPA | After MA: ₹6-12 LPA | After PhD: ₹10-20 LPA
