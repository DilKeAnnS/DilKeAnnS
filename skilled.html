🔧 Skilled & Vocational Careers
🔧 Skilled (Vocational) Stream
🇮🇳 Government Skill Development Programs
• Skill India / PMKVY – Free training in Electrician, Fitter, Welder, Automotive, Plumber, Solar technician, CCTV, Beauty & wellness, Computer operator, Retail & sales
• ITI (Industrial Training Institute) – 1-2 years trades: Electrician, Fitter, Welder, Machinist, Motor Mechanic, Wireman, AC & Refrigeration, Diesel Mechanic, Electronics Mechanic, CNC Operator
• Apprenticeship Program (NAPS) – Paid training in Railways, Metro, Airports, Manufacturing, Shipyards, Power plants
⚙️ Engineering & Technical Skill Diplomas (1-2 years)
• Diploma in Electrical Technician, Mechanical Technician, Fire & Safety, Welding Technology, Tool & Die Making, Draftsmanship (Civil/Mechanical), Automotive Technician, Robotics Technician
• Short certifications: AutoCAD, SolidWorks, PLC Automation, HVAC, Building Electrician, Solar Installation
🛫 International Skilled Jobs (Gulf / Europe / Canada)
• Prepare through certifications: Electrician Licence, Welder (3G/6G), Scaffolding, Fire & Safety (NEBOSH), HVAC Technician, Plumbing Technician, Logistics & Supply Chain Technician
🧪 Skilled Courses in Medical & Allied Fields
• Medical Lab Technician (MLT), X-ray Technician, Operation Theatre Technician, Dialysis Technician, Pharmacy Assistant, Emergency Technician
🧵 Skilled Trades in Non-Technical Sectors
• Beauty & Grooming: Hair stylist, Makeup artist, Spa/beauty therapist
• Culinary & Hospitality: Chef course, Bakery & confectionary, Housekeeping, Hotel operations
• Fashion Skills: Tailoring, Pattern making, Boutique management
• Computer Skills: Data entry, Office management, Graphic design, Web design, Digital marketing
🎨 Creative Skilled Courses
• Photography, Video editing, Animation (2D/3D), Sound engineering, Jewellery design, Interior design (short-term)
🚗 Automotive & Transport
• Automobile mechanic, Two-wheeler mechanic, Diesel engine technician, Heavy vehicle operator, Logistics & warehousing technician
🔥 High-Income Skilled Courses (Quick Jobs + Abroad)
• CNC Operator, Industrial Electrician, Pipe Fitter (Oil & Gas), Welding Specialist (TIG/MIG/ARC), Fire & Safety (International – NEBOSH / IOSH), Marine Technician (Shipping)
💼 Job Opportunities After Skilled Training
• Government departments (Railways, Police, Army, Navy, Air Force, State Govts), Private companies, Factories, Workshops, Self-employment (repair shops, salon, boutique, catering, etc.)
• High demand in Gulf countries, Canada, Australia for skilled trades with good salaries and PR pathways.
